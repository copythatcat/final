Java Town is your new favorite cafe

Java Town is a family-owned cafe that has been serving the community the finest coffee sourced from the Andes. We bake fresh pastries and roast our own beans in-house. While we may not be the quickest coffee shop out there, we pride ourselves on quality and making the best cup of joe.


We've been here for 10 years, and we hope to serve you a fresh cup of joe for another next 10 years. Come on by and make us a part of your morning routine.
